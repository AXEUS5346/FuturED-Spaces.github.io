Best Practices & Guidelines

Effective Communication in Practice

Do's

  • Address conflicts promptly
  • Maintain neutral body language
  • Focus on the issue, not the person
  • Validate emotions and concerns
  • Document agreements and follow up
Preventing Conflict Escalation

Don'ts

  • Take sides or show bias
  • Make assumptions
  • Let emotions control responses
  • Rush to conclusions
  • Ignore underlying issues

  • Options - Explore possibilities
  • Way Forward - Create action plan
